On the Subject of Wind-Up Maze

/!\ IMPORTANT /!\
This is a module in the Wind-Up series. Refer to Appendix WIND-UP KEY for important notes.

The module holds a RESET, SUBMIT, and TURN key hole, as well as a button. To solve, find your starting coords, target coords, and which maze to use.

The starting and target coords can be found using the LED group on the top left square and the keyhole to its right. Up shows the start lights, right shows the target lights.
The white light is the base coords. for each red light in the position below, move the base around

RIGHT D Batts → ↓ AA Bats DOWN
→ Nums in SN Lets in SN ↓
↓ 3rd in SN 6th in SN →
DOWN Lit Ind ↓ → Unlit Ind RIGHT

To determine what maze you're in, you'll need to check what indicators you have then follow the steps below until you're left with one indicator:

  • If there are no indicators, use the █████ maze
  • Using all LIT indicators, stop at the first label found in the list below
  • If there are no LIT indicators, use the UNLIT indicators instead
SND IND FRK FRQ NSA MSA TRN CLR SIG BOB CAR

Coords start at the upper left corner and follow (x,y), with y moving downwards. You always start facing up.

RESET: resets your position to the starting coords and faces you UP.
TURN: Turns your direction left or right.
CHECK: Submit the current coords.
The arrow button will move you forward one space.
